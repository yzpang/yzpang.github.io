Hello. I am a fourth-year (undergraduate) student majoring in computer science and mathematics at the University of Chicago (and a proud resident of Dodd-Mead House in Burton-Judson Courts). Get in touch at yz at uchicago dot edu!

My research focuses on natural langauge processing and machine learning. Recent interests include (i) learning and inference in structured prediction, (ii) multi-view learning and information theoretic co-training, and (iii) controlled text generation. I am actively exploring other subfields.

My advisor is Professor Kevin Gimpel at Toyota Technological Institute at Chicago (TTIC) and the University of Chicago. I have been a member of the TTIC Speech and Language Group since September 2017.
